Professional Football Pitch

Our home ground at DYCCC Yemen Grounds provides a professional training environment with well-maintained grass pitch, proper markings, and adequate space for all training activities.

  • Full-size football pitch - Proper dimensions for match play
  • Well-maintained grass surface - Safe for training and matches
  • Professional markings - Clear boundaries and penalty areas
  • Adequate lighting - For evening training when needed
  • Secure perimeter - Safe environment for young players

    How to Find Us

    • Address:
      DYCCC Yemen Grounds, Chang'ombe
      Opposite DUCE University
      Dar es Salaam, Tanzania
    • Public Transport:
      Daladala Route 106 to Chang'ombe
      Stop at DUCE University, cross to Yemen Grounds
    • By Car:
      From City Center → Nelson Mandela Road → Chang'ombe Road
      Look for DUCE University on your right, ground is opposite
    • Parking:
      Ample parking available on-site
      Secure parking area for parents
