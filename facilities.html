Our Facilities

Professional training environment at DYCCC Yemen Grounds

World-Class Training Facilities

FirstLight Academy trains at the prestigious DYCCC Yemen Grounds, one of Dar es Salaam's premier football facilities. Our location provides the perfect environment for youth development with professional-grade infrastructure.

Location Advantages:

  • Centrally located in Chang'ombe
  • Opposite DUCE University for easy access
  • Secure and gated premises
  • Ample parking for parents
  • Shaded viewing areas

Facility Features

Professional Football Pitch

Well-maintained grass pitch with proper markings for training sessions and matches.

Modern Training Equipment

Complete set of training equipment including cones, hurdles, ladders, and agility poles.

Safe Environment

Secure training grounds with first aid facilities and certified safety protocols.

Hydration Stations

Clean drinking water available throughout training sessions for player hydration.

Equipment Provided

Training bibs, balls, and basic equipment provided for all sessions.

Medical Support

First aid trained staff and emergency medical protocols in place.

Safety & Security

At FirstLight Academy, the safety and well-being of our players is our top priority.

Safety Measures:

  • Secure, gated premises with controlled access
  • First aid trained staff available at all sessions
  • Adequate hydration and sun protection protocols
  • Emergency action plan and contacts
  • All coaches background checked and certified
