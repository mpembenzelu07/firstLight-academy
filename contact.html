Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you accept?

We accept players from 4 to 17 years old, divided into four age groups: U6 (4-6), U10 (7-10), U14 (11-14), and U17 (15-17).

What are the training days and times?

Training is every Saturday and Sunday. Morning sessions: 7:30-9:30 AM, Afternoon sessions: 3:30-5:30 PM. See our Schedule page for details.

What documents are required for registration?

Required documents: 1) Birth certificate copy, 2) 2 passport photos, 3) Medical certificate, 4) School report copy.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes! We offer FREE trial sessions every weekend. You can book a trial through our website or by calling/WhatsApping us.

What is included in the monthly fee?

Monthly fee includes: Professional coaching, training equipment, facility usage, and tournament participation. Training kit is purchased separately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa), bank transfer (CRDB, NMB), and cash payments at our office.
