Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about registration and training

What are the age requirements for joining the academy?

We accept players from 4 to 14 years old, divided into three age groups: U6 (4-6 years), U10 (7-10 years), and U14 (11-14 years). Each group has tailored training programs suitable for their developmental stage.

How much does it cost to join FirstLight Academy?

We offer competitive pricing with monthly, quarterly, and annual payment options. Fees include professional coaching, training equipment, and facility maintenance. Contact us directly for current rates and any available scholarships.

Do you offer trial sessions?

Yes! We offer one FREE trial session for all new players. This allows you to experience our coaching style, meet the trainers, and see if our academy is the right fit before committing. Book your trial through the registration form.

What equipment does my child need?

Basic requirements include football boots, shin guards, comfortable sports clothing, and water bottle. The academy provides training balls, cones, and other equipment. We can recommend affordable local suppliers for gear.

What happens in case of bad weather?

We have indoor facilities or covered areas for training during light rain. In case of severe weather, sessions may be rescheduled or moved indoors. Parents will be notified via WhatsApp or phone call at least 2 hours before the session.
