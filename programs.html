Training Programs

Comprehensive development programs for all age groups

Our Age Groups

Tailored training programs for each developmental stage

U6 Little Stars

Introduction to Football

Fun-based learning focusing on basic motor skills, ball familiarity, and social development through playful exercises.

  • Basic ball control exercises
  • Motor skills development
  • Fun & engaging sessions
  • Social interaction games
  • Building confidence
  • Play-based learning

Age: 4-6 years

Focus: Fun, Fundamentals, Social Skills

U10 Development Squad

Skill Foundation

Building fundamental techniques, introducing basic tactics, and developing game intelligence through structured training.

  • Technical skills development
  • Basic tactical understanding
  • Small-sided games
  • Coordination & agility
  • Teamwork fundamentals
  • Basic match analysis

Age: 7-10 years

Focus: Technique, Tactics, Teamwork

U14 Elite Squad

Advanced Development

Advanced technical training, tactical sophistication, position-specific skills, and competitive match experience.

  • Advanced techniques
  • Tactical awareness
  • Competitive matches
  • Performance analysis
  • Position-specific training
  • Strength & conditioning

Age: 11-14 years

Focus: Advanced Skills, Competition, Specialization

Program Features

Structured Curriculum

Progressive training program following international youth development standards.

Progress Tracking

Regular assessments and performance tracking with detailed feedback.

Competitive Platform

Regular matches and tournaments against other academies and clubs.

Holistic Development

Focus on character building, discipline, teamwork and leadership skills.

Our Training Methodology

At FirstLight Academy, we follow a player-centered approach that focuses on individual development within a team environment.

Key Principles:

  • Age-appropriate training sessions
  • Individual skill development
  • Small-sided games for decision making
  • Positive reinforcement and feedback
  • Match analysis and tactical understanding
