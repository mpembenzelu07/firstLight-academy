U6 Little Stars Program

Our introductory program focuses on making football fun while developing basic motor skills and coordination. Designed specifically for our youngest players, this program creates a love for the game through playful exercises and positive reinforcement.

Motor Skill Development

Basic coordination, balance, and movement patterns

Ball Familiarity

Introduction to ball control through fun games

Social Skills

Learning to play with others and follow instructions

Fun First Approach

Creating positive experiences with football

Key Focus Areas:

  • Basic dribbling and ball control
  • Simple passing and receiving
  • Spatial awareness games
  • Introduction to scoring goals
  • Fun-based agility exercises
Ages 7-10
2 Hours per Session

U10 Development Program

Building on fundamentals, this program introduces technical skills and basic tactical concepts. Players develop better coordination, learn team play, and start understanding the game's basic principles in a supportive environment.

Technical Skills

Dribbling, passing, shooting, and ball control techniques

Game Understanding

Basic positional awareness and decision making

Physical Development

Agility, coordination, and basic fitness training

Teamwork Skills

Learning to work together and support teammates

Key Focus Areas:

  • Advanced ball control skills
  • Passing accuracy and timing
  • Basic defensive principles
  • Small-sided game tactics
  • Position-specific basics
Ages 11-14
2 Hours per Session

U14 Academy Program

Our advanced program focuses on competitive development, tactical understanding, and position-specific training. Players prepare for higher levels of competition while continuing to develop technical excellence and game intelligence.

Tactical Development

Advanced game strategies and positional play

Position Specialization

Developing skills for specific playing positions

Competitive Experience

Regular matches and tournament participation

Advanced Fitness

Strength, speed, and endurance training

Key Focus Areas:

  • Advanced tactical understanding
  • Position-specific skills development
  • Match analysis and strategy
  • Leadership and communication skills
  • Competitive match preparation

Our Curriculum

Comprehensive development approach for all age groups

Technical Skills

Ball mastery, passing accuracy, shooting technique, dribbling skills, and first touch development.

Tactical Understanding

Game strategies, positional awareness, decision making, and reading of the game.

Physical Development

Speed, agility, coordination, strength, and endurance training appropriate for each age.

Mental Skills

Concentration, confidence, resilience, and leadership development.
