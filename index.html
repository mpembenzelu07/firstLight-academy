Why Choose FirstLight Academy

We provide a comprehensive football development program focused on technical skills, physical fitness, and character development.

Professional Coaching

Our coaches are CAF and FIFA licensed with extensive experience in youth development and competitive football training.

Prime Location

Located at DYCCC Yemen Grounds, Chang'ombe - opposite DUCE University. Easy access with ample parking and public transport.

Age-Specific Training

Specialized programs for U6, U10, and U14 age groups with progressive curriculum designed for each development stage.

Free Trial Sessions

Tournament Participation

Regular participation in local leagues and tournaments to give players competitive experience and exposure.

Holistic Development
