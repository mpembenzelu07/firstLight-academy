Our Age Groups

Professional training programs tailored for each developmental stage

U14 Elite Squad

Competitive Development

Advanced training focusing on tactical understanding, position-specific skills, and competitive match experience.

  • Competitive matches & tournaments
  • Advanced tactical training
  • League participation
  • Team building exercises
U10 Development Squad

Skill Foundation

Building fundamental skills, introducing basic tactics, and developing a love for the game through fun exercises.

  • Fundamental skill development
  • Coordination & agility
  • Fun-based learning
  • Small-sided games
U6 Little Stars

Introduction to Football

Fun-first approach focusing on basic motor skills, ball familiarity, and social development through play.

  • Ball familiarity exercises
  • Motor skill development
  • Fun & engaging sessions
  • Building confidence
