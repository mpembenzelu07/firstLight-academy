About FirstLight Academy

Learn about our mission, philosophy, and commitment to developing Tanzania's next generation of football talent

Our Mission

To provide professional football training that develops technical skills, builds character, and instills discipline in young athletes, preparing them for both sporting and life success.

Our Vision

To become Tanzania's premier youth football academy, recognized for developing well-rounded athletes who excel both on and off the pitch, contributing to the growth of Tanzanian football.

Our Philosophy

We believe in a holistic approach to youth development

Technical Excellence

Professional coaching focused on developing fundamental skills, tactical understanding, and game intelligence.

Character Building

Developing discipline, respect, teamwork, and leadership skills that extend beyond football.

Teamwork & Community

Fostering a supportive environment where players learn to work together and support each other.

Education Balance

Emphasizing the importance of academic success alongside athletic development.

Our Story

Founded in 2017, FirstLight Football Academy was established with a clear vision: to provide professional football training for young athletes in Dar es Salaam. Starting with just 15 players, we have grown into one of the city's premier youth football academies, serving over 200 players across three age groups.

Our journey began at the DYCCC Yemen Grounds in Chang'ombe, where we recognized the need for structured, professional training for young football enthusiasts. What started as a weekend training program has evolved into a comprehensive development system that focuses on technical skills, physical fitness, and character development.

Today, FirstLight Academy stands as a testament to our commitment to youth development. We take pride in seeing our players grow not just as footballers, but as responsible young individuals who carry the values of discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship into all aspects of their lives.
