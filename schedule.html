Holiday Schedule

Training breaks and special holiday sessions

Training Breaks

FirstLight Academy follows the school calendar year. We take breaks during major school holidays to allow players time to rest and recharge.

No Training On:

  • Public Holidays - All national holidays
  • School Holiday Weeks - Mid-term and long breaks
  • Inclement Weather - For safety during heavy rain

Note: Parents will receive advance notice of any schedule changes via WhatsApp and email.
